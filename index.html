By applying Alternate Wetting and Drying Technique (AWD) and 1M5R in Rice cultivation, there is a possibility to reduce 7,5 million tonnes CO2e annually in Mekong Delta River.

Who We Are

We are a group of Agriculture scientist, Software developers and Agronomist who share a common interest: to help Farmers apply modern cultivation technique with the help of modern technologies to improve their livelihood and reduce GHG emissions.

Approach

Our technology helps to digitalize the farming practices and measure GHG emissions. We communicate with Cooperatives and Farmers group using scientific data to illustrate the situations and give the recommendations.

Setup Experiment Center

We partner with Research institutes/scientists in the region we are going to to setup experiment plots. This helps to optimize the use of AgriInputs and defines baseline practices.

Apply Changes

Farmers to practice according to the baseline practices with support from our experts.

Measure GHG Emissions

CH4, CO2, N2O and other factors are measured regularly to calculate GHG emissions.

Our Technology

Our self-produced portable and stand-alone IoT devices are designed and built to steadily work under extreme conditions. They are installed directly into the fields and transmitted required data back to the server. This is to help our scientists and experts stay informed in real-time.

Water level reading

A simple solution to replace the traditional pani-tube which helps to get accurate water level on a cost effective and runs on its own for up to 6 months.

Ambient, Soil reading

Humidity, Temperature, Heat index, Light intensify, N, P, K, Moisture, etc ...

Portable GHG reading

A break-thru solution for GHGs directly reading. It saves cost and able to scale
