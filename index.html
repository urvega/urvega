OUR STORY

Our story began with a vision to grow crops at home after seriously concerns about the quality of the product we bought from the market. And the aim is just to feed our families with a clean and green vegetables.

  • 2018 - We tried out the Hydroponics models. Everything is manual from preparing and pumping in the fertilizer solutions and everything it takes to grow crops.
  • Late 2018 – We started using timers to take care of the tasks like: pumping in the nutritient, turning on/off for the LED spectrum.
  • Early 2019 – We applied Internet of Things (IoT) to our model.
  • Late 2019 – We came up with two designs for urban farming
  • Early 2020 – We started including Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence to let machine handle taks
  • Late 2020 – We started rolling out to friends, families an partners
